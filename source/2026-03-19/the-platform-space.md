# The Platform Space — Alex Craciun
**Date:** Thu, Mar 19, 2026
**Subject:** Part 1: I've Been a Platform PM for 6 Years — I'm Open-Sourcing Everything I Know

## Summary
Alex Craciun, a Platform PM with 6 years of experience, is building a Claude agent to externalize and open-source all her platform PM knowledge — frameworks, mental models, templates, outcomes (£1.6M ARR, 90% deployment time reduction).

Key points:
- Built a CLAUDE.md knowledge base capturing 6 years of Platform PM experience across 4 very different environments
- Includes real outcomes with numbers, decision frameworks, discovery templates, career context
- Building it forced explicitness about tacit knowledge ("writing it down was as valuable as having it")
- Agents struggled where the knowledge was unclear — prompting as feedback loop
- Series: Part 2 will cover building skills into the agent; Part 3 covers what to delegate vs. keep
- An MVP Claude agent is available for the community
