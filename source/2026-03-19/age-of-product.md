# Age of Product — Stefan Wolpers
**Date:** Thu, Mar 19, 2026
**Subject:** Three Claude Skills That Make You Harder to Replace

## Summary
Stefan Wolpers releases three free Claude Skills (structured AI protocols) to turn Claude into a structured thinking partner for agile practitioners — not for output speed, but for judgment quality.

Key points:
- **Socratic Explorer**: three-phase problem exploration (territory → principles → your specific situation). Forces understanding before solving.
- **Brutal Critic**: stress-tests plans and strategies. Identifies the load-bearing assumption, what you're avoiding, and what someone who already succeeded would do differently.
- **Pre-Mortem**: assumes failure, works backward. Ranks three most likely failure causes, names a 30-day tripwire metric.
- Based on research: imagining failure increases identification of reasons by 30% (Mitchell, Russo, Pennington)
- Download: ZIP file with three .skill files + installation guide
- Also announces: ClawCamp Hamburg (June 2026), AI for Agile BootCamp #7 (May 28–June 25)
