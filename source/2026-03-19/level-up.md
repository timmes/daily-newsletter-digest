**Subject:** Get the Most Out of Your Performance Review

## Summary
11-step guide to owning your performance review, based on Ethan Evans's 27+ years including 15 at Amazon managing up to 800 employees.

Key steps (free portion):
1. **Clarity is the goal** — Not validation; know what you're doing well AND what to fix
2. **Don't wait for the annual review** — Build a year of small feedback loops; recency bias is real
3. **Own the context when managers change** — Proactively connect new managers with people who know your work
4. **Align performance with motivation** — Transition to engaging work early in the year
